# awarenessai
AwarenessAI helps businesses become the brands AI systems understand, trust, and recommend through Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO). Our AI Recommendation Optimisation (AIRO) methodology improves how companies are represented and ranked by AI tools including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity.
## What AwarenessAI does

AwarenessAI works with businesses, public bodies, and large organisations to improve how they are represented and recommended by AI systems such as ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity.

We focus on how AI interprets brand information across websites, structured data, trusted platforms, and third-party sources.
## What is GEO and AIRO?

**Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)** is the practice of improving how generative AI systems interpret, summarise, and present information about a brand.

**AI Recommendation Optimisation (AIRO)** focuses specifically on increasing the likelihood that AI systems recommend a brand when users ask comparative or decision-making questions.
