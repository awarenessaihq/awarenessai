AI visibility and GEO
AI visibility describes how clearly and consistently a brand is understood, cited and recommended across AI search platforms.
Generative Engine Optimisation, or GEO, is the process of improving the content, structure, authority and digital signals that help generative AI systems understand and surface a brand.
AwarenessAI uses plain-English AI visibility terminology commercially, while GEO remains part of the wider technical and educational framework.
